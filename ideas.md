# Deferred Ideas

Keep the first build focused on a complete, working coaching platform. These ideas are intentionally deferred:

- Native Expo iOS client that consumes the same Supabase schema.
- Stripe billing and subscription management.
- Push notifications through Expo/Apple Push Notification service.
- AI-generated meal plan suggestions.
- Wearable integrations for sleep, steps, and workout imports.
- Video hosting/upload pipeline beyond external links.
- Community image uploads and freeform member post tags.
- Advanced analytics dashboards for cohorts and revenue.
